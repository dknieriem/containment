Dismissable notifications. Can be warnings of poor conditions, actionable items.

Examples of notifications:
Sector 2,5 has damage, and no builders are assigned to repair

Notification Possibilities:
* Group member low on health, not a patient
* Group member low on health, patient, no doctor
* Dangerous relationship score between two group members
* Incoming horde in visible sector
* No guards in claimed sector
* No looters (no food / supplies increase )
* Damage in sector, no builders
* Low food (will run out at current rationing level within 24 hrs)
