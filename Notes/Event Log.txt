Archived log of event items.

Events contain: 
# Date + Time of occurence.
# Sector occured in
# Person(s) / party / group involved

Example Event in log:
Jul 1, 2014, 10 AM: Completed Fence Building in Sector 13,2
Jul 1, 2014,  3 PM: Steve Johnson joined group in Sector 5,15

Event Possibilities:
* Combat in a sector. Condition: Group has members present in that sector
* Group member death
* Sector Repairs completed
* Sector Building completed
* Group member scout or looter MIA.
* New sector arrivals (asking to join group)
* New Group member accepted
